POHA

description

Poha is a popular Indian breakfast dish made from flattened rice, often flavored with onions, green chilies, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. This light and nutritious dish is typically garnished with peanuts, cilantro, and a splash of lemon juice, offering a delightful balance of flavors and textures. It's a quick and easy-to-make dish enjoyed across India for its delicious taste and versatility.

Ingredients

  • Poha (Flattened Rice): 2 cups
  • Oil: 2-3 tablespoons
  • Mustard Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Green Chilies: 2-3, finely chopped
  • Curry Leaves: 8-10 leaves
  • Onion: 1 medium-sized, finely chopped
  • Potatoes: 1 medium-sized, boiled and diced (optional)
  • Peanuts: 1/4 cup, roasted (optional)
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: To taste
  • Sugar: 1/2 teaspoon (optional)
  • Lemon Juice: 1-2 tablespoons
  • Fresh Cilantro Leaves: 2 tablespoons, chopped (for garnish)
  • Fresh Grated Coconut: 2 tablespoons (optional, for garnish)

steps

  1. Rinse 2 cups of poha (flattened rice) in a colander under running water for a few seconds. Drain and set aside. Be gentle to avoid breaking the poha.
  2. Finely chop onions, green chilies, and fresh cilantro leaves.
  3. Boil and dice potatoes if using.
  4. Heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil in a pan or kadhai over medium heat.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds and 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds to the hot oil.
  6. Once the seeds start to splutter, add 2-3 finely chopped green chilies and 8-10 curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
  7. Add 1 medium-sized finely chopped onion to the pan. Sauté until translucent.
  8. If using boiled potatoes, add them to the pan and sauté for another minute.
  9. Add the rinsed and drained poha to the pan.
  10. Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, salt to taste, and optionally, 1/2 teaspoon of sugar over the poha. Gently mix to combine.
  11. Cover the pan and let the poha cook for 3-4 minutes on low heat. This allows the flavors to blend and the poha to become soft.
  12. Once the poha is cooked, squeeze 1-2 tablespoons of lemon juice over it.
  13. Garnish with roasted peanuts, fresh chopped cilantro leaves, and grated coconut if desired.
  14. Serve hot and enjoy this delicious and nutritious breakfast dish!
